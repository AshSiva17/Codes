Instructions

Beginning

This is a 2 player game. One player is X and the other is O. Players take turns placing their mark (X or O) on an empty square. Click on an empty space to place your mark

Objective

To win a round, be the first player to get three marks in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally) on a 3x3 grid. If all 9 squares are filled and there is not a winner, the round is a draw. Click the "new round" button at the end of each round.

Score

You can decide on a target score and whoever reaches that score first wins. You can restart scores and play again by clicking the "Restart" button
